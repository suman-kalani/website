Dr.Suman Kalani

Introduction

Dr. Suman Kalani is an erudite academician and legal scholar, currently serving as the Associate Professor and Vice Principal (In-Charge) at SVKM’s Pravin Gandhi College of Law, Mumbai, India. She leads multiple specialized research centers, including the Centre for Corporate Laws and Intellectual Property Rights, the Centre for Conflict Resolution, and the Centre for Robotics, AI, and Technology Law. With expertise in Contract Law, Intellectual Property Rights, Cyber Laws, and Property Laws, Dr. Kalani is astutely engaged in teaching and research. She has conceptualized and developed various courses, seminars, and competitions addressing contemporary legal issues, demonstrating her commitment to academic innovation. Dr. Kalani holds a law degree from Government Law College, Mumbai, an LL.M. from the Department of Law, University of Mumbai, and a Ph.D. from SNDT University. Her doctoral research on Online Dispute Resolution (ODR) is recognized for its trailblazing approach, including the development of an ODR model for consumer and family disputes. She has also done her Post Graduate Diploma In Human Rights from Department of Civics and Politics, University of Mumbai and Post Graduate Diploma in Cyber Laws from National Law University, Hyderabad.She has authored course books on Business Laws and Intellectual Property Rights. Her passion lies in coordinating the themes between legal theory and practice, actively fostering industry-academia collaborations to provide students with real-world legal exposure. Entailing the administrative post she has contributed in providing mediation training at the grassroots level through various NGOs, furthering her commitment to access to justice and dispute resolution.
